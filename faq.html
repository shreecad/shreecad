FAQ...

What is the eligibility for attending the training program?

Professionals working in the industry in Maintenance department, in Commissioning department, Technicians, fresh graduates and students of science and engineering streams who intend to build career in high growth automation industry.

I don't know anything about industrial automation. Do you think I will be able to grasp the knowledge?

Yes. We start the training from basic and cover the course in details, so it should not be a problem for you to understand. We expect you to be able to solve all the routine automation related problems after completion of the course.

What is the duration of the course?

  • 30 Days only. (Morning / Evening session classes)
  • 45 Days only. Week end classes (Saturday & Sunday classes)
  • 15 Days only. Four Hours in a Day (Crash course / Short term courses)
  • 6 Days only. (Full time classes)
We have successfully conducted many batches in which more than 200 professionals working in the industry as well as fresh graduates have attended our course and they are satisfied with the same. We do cover theory but more emphasis is given on Practical. We also arrange additional courses to meet the specific needs of our customers. Please contact us if the timing & venue shown are not convenient for your training.

Do you provide a hands-on training?

Yes we provide a system for each participant. Also we take only 10 participants in a batch to have personalised attention. To enhance the effectiveness, the course is highly interactive.

Is this course applicable for Instrumentation/Electronics/Electrical engineers only?

No. This training is useful for all the professionals who are working in the industry as industrial automation covers all the divisions of the plant.

I am a chemical/mechanical/production engineer. How will this course help me?

Automation has become an integral part of process operation. As you will appreciate, the Indian and global manufacturing sector is rapidly embracing the automation of their processes to enhance productivity and better quality. This is a high growth sector globally. It is essential to all professionals to have practical knowledge about the automation hardware and software.

What I will be gaining after completion of this course?

You will be enriched with the latest technical knowledge related with the industrial automation. You will be in a position to design and commission an automation system.

Do you provide a job guarantee?

Depending upon the requirement from the industry, we send the participants for interviews. As a premium training provider in the automation industry and with strong track record of executing automation projects, we receive a good number of inquiries from the industry but this is highly time dependent. We do not guarantee jobs but can assist you in getting a job.
